# Privacy Policy for Daily Zach

## Last Updated: 10/02/24

1.  ### Introduction

    Welcome to Daily Zach, a social media app for the consumption of Zach Selfies.
    This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. By using Daily Zach, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1.  ### Information We Collect

    #### Personal Information

        When you use Daily Zach, we may collect the following types of personal information:

        - Name
        - Email Address
        - Images you upload to our services (e.g. Selfie profile image)
        - Your user vertical (the category you select on sign up)
        - Comments

    #### Non-Personal Information

        We may also collect non-personal information, including:

        - Device Information
        - Usage Data (app engagement)
        - Error Logs

1.  ### How We Use Your Information

    We use the collected information for the following purposes:

    - For basic application functionality (e.g. authentication)
    - For enriched application functionality (e.g Selfie profile image)
    - To understand our users and their behaviour
    - To inform future updates
    - To track and fix issues in Daily Zach

1.  ### Data Security

    We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

1.  ### Your Choices

    You have the right to:

    - Access personal information we have collected from you, provided we still store it
    - Request the deletion of all your personal information. We will action these requests in a timely manner.

1.  ### Children's Privacy

    Daily Zach is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

1.  ### Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

1.  ### Contact Us
    If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [dailyzach@zachhuxford.io](mailto:dailyzach@zachhuxford.io).
